About Us

Hello, We are running this campaign towards the dream of providing education to not so privileged children. We are going to provide this kids with food and enabling education for them.
Our goal is to ensure children have access to free and quality education, healthcare, nutrition and a safe and secure environment to grow up in. And this has been possible only with the support and love of volunteers and donors like YOU. Together, we can help ensure a happy, healthy and creative childhood for every child in India.
